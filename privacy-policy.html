Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily submit through our website, including:

  • Contact form submissions (name, email, phone, message)
  • Property and seller details submitted through our "Request an Offer" form
  • Review submissions, including your name, contact details, property address, closing date, rating, and written review
  • Basic analytics data about how visitors use the site (see "Analytics" below)

How We Use Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to inquiries and discuss a potential property purchase
  • Evaluate a property and prepare a possible offer
  • Verify and moderate review submissions
  • Improve our website and services

Review Publication

If you submit a review, only your first name, last initial, city or county, star rating, review text, property type, and approximate closing month/year may be published — and only with your permission. Your full name, property address, phone number, and email address are never published publicly. They are used solely to verify your review against a genuine transaction.
