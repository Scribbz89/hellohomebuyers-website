About Hello Home Buyers

A real estate investment and property acquisition business focused on North Carolina.

Who We Are

Hello Home Buyers is a real estate investment and property acquisition business. We work directly with homeowners who may want or need to sell a property without going through the traditional estate-agent or Realtor process.

We are not presenting ourselves as traditional real estate agents. We are property buyers and real estate investors. We speak directly with property owners, understand their situation, assess the property, and make an offer based on the property's condition, location, market value, repair requirements, and the seller's circumstances.

How We Structure Transactions

Depending on the deal, we may purchase a property ourselves, work with one of our investment partners, or assign our contractual interest to another qualified buyer or investor. The exact structure depends on the transaction and will always be set out in the signed purchase agreement.

For a full explanation of how this works, see our Real Estate Disclosure page.

Focused on North Carolina

Our current primary market is North Carolina, including the following areas and surrounding communities.

Durham Gastonia Hickory Mount Holly Morganton Bessemer City Rougemont Valdese Claremont Charlotte area
